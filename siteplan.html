Site Purpose

The purpose of MyFitness Tracker is to provide users with an easy-to-use tool to log workouts, track progress, and set health goals. The site will include workout logging, goal tracking, and motivational resources to help users stay on track.

Scenarios

  • How can I log a custom workout that includes weightlifting and cardio?
  • Can I view my progress for the last month and compare it to my goals?
